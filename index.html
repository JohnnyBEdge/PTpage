Physical Therapy

We are committed to the patient experience and increased the time that a physical therapist spends with each patient. PRC has always offered a clinical environment that is focused on medically based rehabilitation. We are neither a health club nor an alternative medicine provider. As healthcare dollars are stretched, many providers are using ancillary staff to fill the gaps. We still subscribe to the philosophy that one-on-one interaction with a PRC therapist is crucial to a patient’s rehab experience. We are passionate about understanding each patient’s unique anatomy and how he or she can reach their desired goals.

Our Philosophy

At Performance Rehab Clinic, our philosophy is that one-on-one interaction with a knowledgeable physical therapist is crucial to a patient’s rehab experience. We are passionate about understanding each patient’s unique circumstances and helping them achieve their goals. As we build plans around you and your abilities, we want to focus on your needs and goals.

Sports Medicine

We take pride in providing evidence-based approaches to treatment for an ever-widening list of diagnoses. We are experienced and knowledgeable in sports-related injuries. Performance Rehab Clinic treats athletes at every level and in every discipline; while we have experience treating elite-level athletes, many of our patients are amateur to intermediate athletes as well.

Orthopedic Rehab

Orthopedics is the field of medicine concerned with the musculoskeletal system. We are experienced in treating a broad range of diagnoses, including acute and chronic injuries along with surgical and non-surgical orthopedic cases.
